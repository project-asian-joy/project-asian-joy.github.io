The Thousand Bloom

The Thousand Bloom is a public art project featuring 1,000 silk chrysanthemums to symbolize Boston Chinatown’s resilience, incorporating a grand installation, business support initiatives, and Chinatown’s first ever Double Ninth Festival.

The Thousand Bloom – A Chrysanthemum Grows in Chinatown 千朵花開:唐人街長出一朵菊花 is part of the Un-monument | Re-monument | De-monument: Transforming Boston Project presented by Pao Arts Center, curated by Lani Asunción, and funded by the Mayor’s Office of Arts and Culture, and the City of Boston with the support from the Mellon Foundation.
